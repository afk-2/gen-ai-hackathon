# Hi Grandma 👋

You know how when a company is hiring, someone has to sit down and read through
dozens of CVs to figure out who might be a good fit? That takes a long time.

This tool helps with that.

You upload a CV, and the computer reads it for you and understands it. Then you can:

- Ask it questions like "does this person speak French?" and it will find the answer straight from the CV
- Ask it to pull out the important details — name, skills, work history — in a neat list
- Ask it to give you a short summary of who the person is
- Paste in a job description and it will tell you how well the CV matches, with a score out of 100

The computer only tells you what is actually written in the CV. If something is not there, it says so. It will not make things up.

If the main AI we use is having a bad day, the tool quietly switches to a backup AI so you still get your answer.

That is really all it does. One CV in, useful answers out.

It works best when the CV is typed text. If it’s just a picture or a scanned document, it might not read it properly.

It’s not perfect, but it saves time and helps people quickly understand a CV.
